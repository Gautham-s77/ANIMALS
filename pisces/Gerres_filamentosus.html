Gerres filamentosus (Cuvier, 1829)

Distribution: Indo-Pacific: East Africa and Madagascar to Japan and Australia
Depth Range: 1-50m
Description: 9 Dorsal spines, 10-11 Dorsal soft rays, Anal spines 2-3, Anal soft rays 7-8
Biology: Adults are coastal inhabitants found on soft bottoms, Juveniles are found in brackish mangrove estuaries
Feeding: small crustaceans and polychaetes
